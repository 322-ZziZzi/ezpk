EZPK War Portal v169

Zombie Defense
- Removed duplicate in-canvas barricade HP, wave HUD, WAVE CLEAR, FINAL DEFENSE, and DEFEND text.
- Retained wave, kill count, barricade HP, score, and time in the external toolbar only.
- Added guaranteed bomb pickups at approximately 13s, 30s, and 47s.
- Bomb pickups remain for 4 seconds and have a short 0.2-second mistap lock.
- Bombs instantly remove normal, runner, and poison zombies; giant zombies take 70% of their current HP.
- Bomb kills award reduced points, include a crowd-kill bonus, cap at 5,000 points per bomb, and increase combo by at most 2.
- Added bomb pulse, explosion shake, and phase-change visual/audio cues.

Hero Merge
- Replaced exponential merge scoring with fixed level-based scores.
- Added a 350-point active-move score so roughly 30 seconds of normal play targets about 40,000 points.
- Added combo milestone bonuses at x5, x10, and x15.
- Removed oversized tier-up score bonuses.
- Reduced end-of-game highest-hero bonuses to 3,000 / 5,000 / 8,000 / 12,000 for Lv7-Lv10.
- Reduced auto-merge rescue bonus to prevent score inflation.
